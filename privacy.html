Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Your Privacy is Protected

Sudhir QR is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your data belongs to you and only you. This application is designed to work entirely in your browser without sending any data to external servers.

What We DO

  • Process everything locally in your browser
  • Allow you to save history in local storage
  • Provide secure QR code generation
  • Respect your privacy choices completely
  • Let you clear all data anytime
  • Work offline after initial load

What We DON'T Do

  • Collect or store your data on servers
  • Track your QR code usage or activity
  • Share information with third parties
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Require account or registration
  • Send analytics or telemetry data

1 How It Works

All QR code generation and scanning happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to any server. Your generated QR codes and scanned data remain on your device only. History is stored in your browser's local storage and can be cleared at any time by you.

Client-Side Processing

All operations run in your browser, not on any server

Local Storage Only

History and preferences saved locally in your browser

Works Offline

After loading, works without internet connection

Easy Data Deletion

Clear history anytime with one click

3 Important Security Notes

Credentials & Sensitive Data

While we provide the ability to create QR codes with username/password or other sensitive information, please be aware that this data is encoded but NOT encrypted in the QR code. Anyone who scans the QR code can read this information.

  • • Never share credentials QR codes publicly
  • • Don't post sensitive QR codes on social media
  • • Use only for personal or secure sharing
  • • Consider the risks before creating such QR codes

Scanning Unknown QR Codes

Be cautious when scanning QR codes from unknown sources. They could lead to:

  • • Malicious websites (phishing, malware)
  • • Automatic downloads of harmful files
  • • Unwanted subscriptions or payments
  • • Privacy-invading tracking links
